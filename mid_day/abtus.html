Welcome to the Mid Day Meal Management

Mid-day meal (MDM) is a wholesome freshly-cooked lunch served to children in government and government-aided schools in India. On 28 November 2001, the Supreme Court of India passed a mandate stating, "We direct the State Governments/Union Territories to implement the Mid Day Meal Scheme by providing every child in every Government and Government assisted Primary School with a prepared midday meal."

Aims :

This website aims at simplifying the task of maintaining Mid Day Meal Scheme which has to be done manually by entering records and maintaining ledger daily by the teachers.
It reduce the manual calculation that neeeds to be done daily by the teachers.
It provides a direct communication with the department office to allocate grants automatically.
Latency is reduced .
